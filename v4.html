Memorii
⚠️ v4 preview

Memorii helps you study Chinese hanzi, Japanese kanji, and Korean hangul.

Manage your flashcards library with folders, checkmarks, and favorites. The app comes with basic vocabulary lists to get you started, and offers additional lists for more advanced learners (with in-app purchases). And your library is available on all your devices with iCloud sync.

Practice your vocabulary with customizable slideshows. Ordered or shuffled, based on characters, pronunciation, or meaning. The app also includes a widget to keep your favorites always visible on your Home Screen and help you memorize throughout the day.

Download Memorii for free on the App Store and Mac App Store.
